Utkonos - entry to the market

Project #1024
BRIEF
Utkonos is an online hypermarket with round-the-clock delivery in Moscow and the region. Utkonos helps not to waste time in queues of shops and traffic jams, not to wear heavy purchases and free time for yourself and your family. In the online hypermarket you will find more than 20 000 products: from fresh seasonal fruits and vegetables to a variety of household chemicals. You can order goods on the site or through the mobile application "Utkonos" wherever there is an Internet. You can buy products from home, for a walk with your child, from the office, in traffic jams and even at the summer cottage. It will not take you as much time as a trip to the hypermarket.
The company has more than 3,700 employees and its own fleet of more than 800 vehicles equipped with special chambers to keep food fresh. Our company does not cooperate with intermediaries, so your order will never be lost with an unknown courier.
Every day, the employees of Utkonos ONLINE strive to ensure that shopping brings both joy and pleasure, and most importantly, do not take up the time that you can spend together with family and friends.
